Setting Up Bundler and RVM for a RubyMotion App

May 13th, 2013

Create your rvmrc file via:

$ rvm use 1.9.3@yourgemsetname --create --rvmrc

Add bundler to your Rakefile

require 'bundler'
Bundler.require

Create your Gemfile via:

$ bundle init

Update your Gemfile

source "https://rubygems.org"
gem "bubble-wrap"

Install gems

$ bundle

You’re done.
